About Me


Hi, I am Hanqing Wang - a first-year Ph D candidate of Media Computing and Intelligent Systems Lab, Beijing Institute of Technology - Beijing. I am now advised by Professor Wei Liang and Professor Mingtao Pei. I also collaborate with Professor Lap-Fai(Craig) Yu - director of Design Computing and Extended Reality Group, George Mason University - Virginia. I did my internship at Microsoft Research Asia, VC Group from Mar. 2017 to Nov. 2017, mentored by researcher Jiaolong Yang.

My research interests are Computer Vision/Graphics and Machine Learning.
